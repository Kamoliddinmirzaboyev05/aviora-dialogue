# Product Workflows

## Consent-First Lead Flow

1. A Telegram message is simulated.
2. Trigger matching checks configured keywords and negative terms.
3. Mock AI classifies intent and confidence.
4. An opportunity and permission-request draft are created.
5. A human approves the draft.
6. A mock Telegram send is recorded.
7. The contact grants consent.
8. A product response is generated from approved product data.
9. The conversation is converted into a lead.
10. Analytics reflect the new opportunity, consent, and lead.

## Safety Rule

Product information is blocked unless the conversation has granted consent.
